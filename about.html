My College

The Young Revolution Panther Education Society Tumsar Established in the year 1980 for the purpose of commemorating in the memory of late Lord Lady Indutai the elder loving sister of Shri Sachchidanand H. Fulekar. The Young Revolution Panther runs numerous Law Colleges, B.P.Ed. Colleges, B.Ed. and D.Ed. Colleges, Senior Colleges, Junior Colleges, High Schools and Primary Schools across Vidharbha Region as well as other districts of Maharashtra state. To render distinguished services in the field of education by imparting moral, cultural and academic instructions to the students and citizens of India to provide best tradition and ideal atmosphere which would help to be a worthy citizens of our motherland. The Young Revolution Panther Tumsar Society will endeavour to bring out each of its students as honest and diligent with a sense of dully and responsibility implied in him.

  Objectives

  • To impart legal education so that the students achieves a level of academic excellence.
  • To guide the students in the right direction to ensure their emergence as diligent, competent and knowledgeable individuals.
  • To install a sense of duty towards the family, society and nation in the minds of students.
  • To bring out the latent talent of students and make them all-rounders by activity involving them in the activities of the college debating club, social service league, fine arts society, science forums and other bodies.
  • To help students attain proficiency in sports.
  • To give equal opportunity to all students.
  • To sharpen students' focus and help them gain depth in their chosen area of study to achieve academic excellence.
  • To enable them to prepare for lifelong learning by nurturing independent thinking.
  • To sensitize the students towards the immediate environment and the society at large.
  • To provide a platform to actualize students' talents and encourage them to mould their passion into profession.


  Advantages

  • To dynamic governing body to college gives all out help and co-operation to the students in achieving their goals.
  • The college has a dedicated team of well qualified faculty members always ready to help the students.
